5. Billing & Quota Limits

DDS operates on a tiered usage structure:

A. Daily Free Tier

Every developer account receives a daily free allowance of 5 successful authentication requests across all registered applications. Free usage limits are reset automatically at 12:00 AM daily in accordance with the developer's timezone setting.

B. Postpaid Pay As You Go (PAYG)

Once your daily usage exceeds the 5 free requests, you will be billed for subsequent successful verification requests at a rate of ₹0.50 per successful request. Failed or rejected authentication requests are not billed.

C. Invoicing & Card Authorizations

Usage fees are compiled dynamically and invoiced at the end of the billing cycle (month-end). By attaching a credit card, you authorize Stripe and DDS to automatically charge your card on file for invoice balances.

6. Suspension & Grace Period

We enforce strict payment cycles to maintain our global push-delivery infrastructure:

  • Failed Payments: If automatic charging of your payment card fails, your invoice transitions to Payment Pending status.
  • 7-Day Grace Period: You are granted a grace period of seven (7) calendar days from the billing failure date. Your API keys remain fully functional during this period.
  • Service Suspension: If the invoice remains unpaid after the grace period expires, your developer account is suspended. All authentication requests will be rejected with HTTP code 402 Payment Required and error code PAYMENT_REQUIRED.
  • Instant Restoration: Settling outstanding balances will immediately reactivate all developer applications and credentials automatically.
